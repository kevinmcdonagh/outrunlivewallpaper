h1. OutRun Live Wallpaper for Nexus One

!http://github.com/downloads/kevinmcdonagh/outrunlivewallpaper/outrundl_qrcode.png!

This is a Live Wallpaper for Android featuring the arcade classic OutRun. 
You can navigate left&right across your dashboard and scenery changes depending on the time of Day/Night/ & Sunrise/Sunset.
All of the current scenes are taken from OutRun2019(Copyright of Sega).

h2. Scene Background Timings

* Sunrise: 5am-9am 
* Day: 9am-5pm
* Sunset: 5pm-7pm
* Night: 7pm-5am
